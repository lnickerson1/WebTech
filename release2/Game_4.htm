2019 Stanley Cup Finals Game 4

Game Details

  1. Date: June 3, 2019
  2. Location: Enterprise Center
  3. Starting Goalies:
    1. Boston Bruins: Tuukka Rask
    2. St. Louis Blues: Jordan Binnington¹

Outcome: St. Louis Wins 4-2 (Series: 2-2)

The blues tied up the series at 2-2 with their 4-2 victory over the Bruins.
