2019 Stanley Cup Finals Game 1

Game Details

  1. Date: May 27, 2019
  2. Location: TD Garden
  3. Starting Goalies:
    1. Boston Bruins: Tuukka Rask
    2. St. Louis Blues: Jordan Binnington¹

Outcome: Boston Wins 4-2 (Series: 1-0)

The Boston Bruins rallied back from a 2-0 deficit to beat the St. Louis Blues 4-2 putting them in the lead of the series 1-0.
