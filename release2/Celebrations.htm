2019 Stanley Cup Finals Celebrations

Trophy Presentation

Stanley Cup Presentation: The Stanley Cup was presented to the St. Louis Blues immediately following their victory. Each player got the opportunity to hoist the cup above their head and skate a lap around the rink. The team took pictures with the cup.


Conn Smythe Trophy Presentation: Ryan O'Reilly of the St. Louis Blues was awarded the Conn Smythe Trophy as the most valuable player in the playoffs.


Parade

On June 15, 2019 the St. Louis Blues held their "Stanley Cup Rally Parade" which began at the corner of 18th and Market Street and ended at the corner of Broadway and Market Street. The players road on top of buses, firetrucks and pickup trucks, and walked along the street. Fans lined the entire route waving flags, yelling, dancing, and wearing St. Louis jerseys. At the end of the route some of the players as well as head coach Craig Berube gave speeches under the Gateway Arch.¹



Banner Hanging

On October 2nd,2019 the St. Louis Blues concluded their Stanley Cup victory celebrations by hanging the 2019 Stanley Cup Championship Winners Banner in the Enterprise Center. The players stood in a circle, arms entangled, while a winch lifted the banner in front of the Stanley Cup. The banner came to rest in its permanent location next to the division title banners.²
