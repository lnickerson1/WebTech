2019 Stanley Cup Finals Game 3

Game Details

  1. Date: June 1, 2019
  2. Location: Enterprise Center
  3. Starting Goalies:
    1. Boston Bruins: Tuukka Rask
    2. St. Louis Blues: Jordan Binnington¹

Outcome: Boston Wins 7-2 (Series: 2-1)

The Boston Bruins dominate the St. Louis blues in a 7-2 victory once again taking the lead of the series with their 2-1 lead.

Important Events

  • In the first period Patrice Bergeron scored on a power play resulting from an interference penalty on STL by David Perron.
  • In the second period David Pastrnak scored on a power play resulting from a delay of game penalty served by David Perron due to a failed offside coach's challenge by STL.
  • In the second period Torey Krug scored on a power play resulting from a high-sticking penalty on STL by Colton Parayko.
  • Jake Allen replaced STL goalie Jordan Binnington following the goal by Torey Krug at the end of the second period
  • In the third period Colton Parayko scored on a power play resulting from a roughing penalty on BOS by Zdeno Chara.
  • Near the end of the third period St. Louis pulled their goalie to gain an extra skater however, Boston scored on the empty net.
  • In the third period Marcus Johansson scored on a power play resulting from a slashing penalty on STL by Alex Pietrangelo.¹
