2019 Stanley Cup Finals Game 7

Game Details

  1. Date: June 12, 2019
  2. Location: TD Garden
  3. Starting Goalies:
    1. Boston Bruins: Tuukka Rask
    2. St. Louis Blues: Jordan Binnington¹

Outcome: St. Louis Wins 4-1 (Series End: 4-3)

With 4 straight goals the St. Louis Blues win the Stanley Cup finals 4-1 victory over the Bruins ending the best of seven series 4-3.
