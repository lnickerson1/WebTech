Road to the Finals

2019 "was the Boston Bruins' twentieth appearance in the Stanley Cup Finals, six years after 2013, when the Chicago Blackhawks defeated them in six games. The Bruins last won the Stanley Cup in 2011, their sixth Cup in franchise history."¹ The Bruins finished the regular season with 107 points putting them in second in the Atlantic Division and Eastern Conference, as well as third in the league.³ In the post season the Bruins defeated the Montreal Canadians in seven games in the first round. They moved on to beat the Columbus Blue Jackets 4-2. Finally they were crowned champions of the Eastern Conference after their series sweeping 4-0 victory over the Caroline Hurricanes and moved on to face the St. Louis Blues in the finals.²


Home Arena

The Boston Bruins home arena is the TD Garden, located in Boston, Massachusetts at 100 Legends Way, Boston, MA 02114. This arena is shared with the Boston Celtics, NBA team as well as concerts and other events. It has a "capacity of 19,580 and was opened on September 30, 1995".²
