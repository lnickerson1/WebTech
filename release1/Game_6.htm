2019 Stanley Cup Finals Game 6

Game Details

  1. Date: June 9, 2019
  2. Location: Enterprise Center
  3. Starting Goalies:
    1. Boston Bruins: Tuukka Rask
    2. St. Louis Blues: Jordan Binnington¹

Outcome: Boston Wins 5-1 (Series: 3-3)

The Bruins stay alive with their dominant 5-1 victory of the Blues, forcing a game 7 as they tie the series 3-3.

Important Events

  • In the first period Brad Marchand scored on a 5-3 power play resulting from boarding and delay of game (puck over glass) penalties on STL by Brayden Schenn and Ryan O'Reilly respectively.
  • In the third period "the Blues pulled Binnington in an attempt to score 3 goals to tie the game, but Zdeno Chara sealed the victory for the Bruins finalizing the score at 5–1".¹
